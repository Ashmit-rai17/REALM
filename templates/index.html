AI for smarter land use

REALM analyzes satellite imagery with deep learning to map and classify land use — enabling quick, open-source insights for energy site planning. It uses pretrained deep learning models for precise land-type detection — from barren fields to active mining and renewable energy zones.

Why to use this AI?

High-accuracy models
Pretrained CNNs for land-type detection and grad-CAM visual explanations.
Satellite-ready
Works with common satellite datasets and geoTIFFs — pipeline-ready for batch processing.
Interactive outputs
Produces heatmaps, downloadable masks, and simple visual reports for decision making.
Energy Mapping
Instantly identify zones suitable for solar, wind, or mining projects using predictive analytics.
Open-Source Framework
Built with transparency and reproducibility in mind — customize or extend for your use case.
Sustainable Planning
Empower planners and researchers to choose eco-friendly development zones.
